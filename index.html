FAQ

Questions, answered honestly

Is this medical advice?

No. The Root Code provides wellness and nutritional guidance and is not a substitute for medical care. We recommend consulting your doctor before starting any new supplement, and your protocol is designed to be shared with your healthcare provider.

I take prescription medications. Can I still do this?

Yes — and this is exactly why a practitioner reviews every protocol. The questionnaire asks for all medications and supplements you currently take, and your protocol takes them into account. We strongly recommend reviewing it with your doctor or pharmacist before starting, and for complex situations, please work with your doctor directly.

How is this different from a free online quiz?

Free quizzes exist to sell you products. Your Root Code protocol is built from an in-depth questionnaire and personally reviewed by a practitioner — and we don't sell supplements, so every recommendation is there because you need it, not because it's in stock.

How long until I see changes?

Everyone's different. Protocols are phased over weeks to months, and include progress markers so you can track your own response. The Check-In and Transformation packages exist precisely to adjust course based on your results.

What if I'm pregnant, nursing, or have a serious diagnosis?

The questionnaire screens for this. In some cases we'll recommend working directly with your doctor instead of starting a protocol — and if we can't serve you responsibly, you'll receive a full refund.

Can I buy this for my child?

Not at this time. Protocols are currently available for adults (18+).

What's your refund policy?

If we haven't started reviewing your questionnaire yet, you get a full refund, no questions asked. If your situation requires medical care rather than wellness support, we'll refund you and tell you so honestly.

How do I receive the protocol?

By email, as a private link. It opens on any device — no account, no app, no printing required (though it prints beautifully if you like paper).
